## TL;DR

We respect your privacy. We only collect your first name and state to display on our public counter. We will not share your email with anyone. We will only use it to send you occasional, high-signal updates on the Guard Pact's progress.

## What We Collect

- **First Name & State:** To show public support for the Pact.
- **Email Address:** To send you important updates.

## What We Don't Do

- We **do not** sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
- We **do not** engage in spam or send you unrelated marketing emails.

## Your Rights

You can request to have your information removed from our systems at any time. Just contact us at [contact@guardpact.us](mailto:contact@guardpact.us).

## Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out to [contact@guardpact.us](mailto:contact@guardpact.us).
